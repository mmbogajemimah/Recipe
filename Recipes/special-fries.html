Special-fries

INGREDIENTS

  • 30ml (2Tbsp) cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 500g beef, cubed
  • 500ml (2cups) cooked rice
  • 3 Royco Beef Cubes
  • 2 tomatoes cubed
  • Coriander, to garnish (optional)

METHOD

  1. In a large pan, heat the oil and add the onions for a few minutes until they become translucent.
  2. Add the cubed beef into the pan and stir. Reduce the heat, cover and leave to simmer until beef is tender.
  3. Once the meat is cooked, add the cooked rice. Dissolve the Royco Beef Cubes in about 4 tablespoons of hot water and add to the rice and beef. Stir until blended in.
  4. Stir in the tomatoes and garnish with coriander before serving.
